RICS Level 3 Competency Application


Our project incorporates Level 3 professional competencies from the RICS APC pathway. These include the ability to provide reasoned, evidence-based advice using interdisciplinary knowledge applied to real-world development challenges.



  • Managing Projects: Coordinated lifecycle delivery, timelines, and interdependency mapping.
  • Design Economics and Cost Planning: Prepared detailed cost estimates, allowances, and forecast models.
  • Legal and Regulatory Compliance: Addressed zoning, fire safety, environmental and energy compliance standards.
